✨ About Me

Hey there! I'm Tejas Sharma, also known as CYBO (Coolest Young Brain Online) — a passionate learner and explorer on a mission to turn curiosity into creation.

I enjoy blending science, tech, and imagination to build meaningful things both in the real and digital worlds.

🎯 My Goals

My biggest aspiration is travelling across all of India documenting every city, experience, and culture as part of a lifelong creative project.

Through this journey, I aim to capture the essence of India's diversity and share it with the world.

🧩 Hobbies

  • 🧲 Physics – My first love; understanding how the universe works fuels my curiosity.
  • 🚵 MTB Cycling – For pushing boundaries and exploring new paths.
  • 🎶 Music – A powerful source of focus, vibes, and emotion.
  • 🎮 Gaming – Especially Minecraft parkour for fun, flow, and challenge.

🧠 My Mindset

I believe in learning by doing. My approach is simple: stay curious, ask questions, and build things that matter.

Science isn't just about textbooks — it's about seeing the world through a lens of wonder. Technology isn't just tools — it's a canvas for imagination.
